Paragraph and Paragraph Elements

In HTML, spaces and new lines are ignored. The following demonstrates this.

My Bonny lies over the ocean. My Bunny lies over the sea. My Bunny lies over the ocean. Oh, bring back my Bunny to me!

My Bonny lies over the ocean.

Oh, bring back my Bunny to me!

The pre tag preserves both spaces and line breaks. This is demonstrated as follows:

My Bonny lies over the ocean.
		
			My Bunny lies over the sea.
			
				My Bunny lies over the ocean.
				
				Oh, bring back my Bunny to me!

This is
a paragraph
with line breaks
